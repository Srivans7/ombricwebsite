FIELD TRIPS & EXPEDITIONS

Exploring Marine Ecosystems

OMBRIC organizes regular field trips and research expeditions to explore Odisha's rich marine ecosystems. These expeditions provide hands-on experience in marine science, specimen collection, and environmental monitoring.

Our field programs engage researchers, students, and stakeholders in understanding and conserving marine biodiversity.
